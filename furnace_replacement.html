Air Furnace Replacement

If you are having constant problems with your air conditioner, it may be time to replace it. It makes more financial sense to get an air conditioning replacement if you are constantly having to put money into repairs for your old air conditioner.

At HVAC Heating and Air Conditioning we thoroughly evaluate your current air conditioner to see what the problem is and can help you determine if you’re better off with a new AC installation. We make sure that we are transparent throughout the entire process so that you are confident in your AC replacement decision.

Financial Benefits of Replacing Your Old Air Conditioner

You could be losing money on your old AC unit if it needs costly repairs time after time. If you’ve spent more than half the cost of a new AC unit on repairs in the last couple of years, you will be saving money by getting an air conditioning replacement!

A new AC unit will help you save money in a couple of different ways. It will:

· Eliminate Repair Costs — a new system shouldn’t need any repairs for at least 5-6 years.

· Improve Energy Efficiency — energy efficiency standards are getting better all the time, meaning a new air conditioner will give you the same comfort while using less power.
