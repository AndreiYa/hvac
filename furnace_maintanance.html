Heater/Furnace maintenance

Why do I need regular maintenance on my heating system?

Regular furnace maintenance by a reliable contractor like HVAC Heating and Air Conditioning is an important factor in preparing your system to perform at its best. Regular service is vital if you want to avoid unnecessary equipment failure and repair costs. It’s a good idea to have a HVAC technician check and service yours every spring and fall.

How often should I change my furnace air filter?

We recommend changing your disposable filter every 3 months. Likewise, if you have washable filters, you should clean them every 3 months. A filter that’s become clogged with dust, pet dander, mold, pollen, or any number of pollutants and particles puts a strain on your cooling system and spoils the air your family breathes.
